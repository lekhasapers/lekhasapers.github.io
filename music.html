music&noise

I've been studying the modular synthesizer since 2023. I discovered it at UCSB's media arts and technology program in the basement of the music building. Once I graduated and no longer had access to the basement Eurorack, I purchased my own Shared System. I play every day because I'm drawn to the motion: every change is an exercise in experimentation.

Before modular, I focused on DJing. I've spent countless evenings curating and playing, introducing Isla Vistans to Detroit house, New York boom bap. But beyond pure performance, I host a number of conversations on my SoundCloud. There are live sets, interviews with artists, and other sonic pleasures.

I've fallen in love with the sounds around me - I figure the best way to celebrate that is to share them.
