Hey — I'm Lekha

Lekha Sapers is an archivist, programmer, and researcher from Jamestown, Rhode Island.

She is a graduate of UC Santa Barbara, where she studied Global Studies and Earth Science while engaging in research in linguistics, human sexuality, media theory, privacy policy, and digital preservation. She specializes in blending technical skills with creative storytelling, through its many applications.

Lekha’s work spans from developing Omeka-S digital archives for cultural institutions to exploring the intersection of art, privacy, and technology. Currently, she is cataloging and digitizing collections at the Rhode Island Computer Museum while balancing creative pursuits and interdisciplinary media.

In her free time, she focuses on biking, modular synthesis, and chess puzzles. She also has a penchant for poetry and the avant-garde.
