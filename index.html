hey — i'm lekha

Lekha Sapers (/ˈleɪ.kʰɑː ˈseɪ.pɚz/) is a communications specialist, archivist, and researcher from Rhode Island.

She is a graduate of UC Santa Barbara, where she studied Global Studies and Earth Science while engaging in research in linguistics, human sexuality, media theory, privacy policy, and digital preservation. She specializes in blending technical skills with creative storytelling, through its many applications.

Lekha’s work lies within the intersections of art, technology, and communication. Currently, she is cataloging archival collections at the Rhode Island Computer Museum.

In her free time, she focuses on sailing, modular synthesis, and chess puzzles. She also has a penchant for poetry and the avant-garde.
